### Illegal or harmful content
Don't upload, share, or create anything that's illegal under UK or EU law, or the laws of the countries where our third-party services operate. This includes content that promotes terrorism, facilitates crime, infringes others' rights, or is otherwise unlawful.

### Adult content
No pornography or sexually explicit material of any kind. The platform is accessible to users as young as 13.

### Harmful behaviour
No harassment, bullying, threatening, or abusing other users. No content that promotes or glorifies violence, self-harm, or extremism.

### Security attacks
Don't attempt to hack, probe, or disrupt the platform or any other system. Don't try to bypass security controls or access other users' accounts. Don't upload malware with the intent to harm or distribute it to others — but see the research exception below.

### Abuse of features
Don't use AI tools to generate content that breaks these rules. Don't create multiple accounts to get around a ban. Don't hammer the platform with automated requests.

### WebPC specifically
WebPC runs **entirely in your browser** — it's client-side, and we have no visibility into or control over what you run inside it. That also means we're not responsible for it.

Don't use it to attack external systems, scan networks, mine cryptocurrency, or do anything that abuses shared infrastructure. VM data may be deleted at any time, especially for inactive or misused environments. You are solely responsible for what you run in the WebPC environment.

### Security research and malware samples
Uploading malware samples or exploit code to the Content Hub is **allowed for personal educational or security research purposes only.** The rules are simple:

- It's for your own study and analysis — not to share with or deliver to others
- You're not using it as part of an active attack on anything or anyone
- You're not using 332 App as a distribution point for malicious files

All uploads are scanned by VirusTotal. Abuse of this exception will result in an immediate ban and may be reported to authorities.

### Copyright
Don't distribute content that infringes someone else's copyright. Uploading music or media for **private, personal use** is fine. If we get a credible copyright complaint, we'll remove the content without prior notice.

### Our position on age verification

We will **never** ask you to verify your age using:
- Government-issued ID
- Face scans or biometric checks
- Identity document uploads
- Any other privacy-invasive method

We believe these approaches are disproportionate harms in their own right. Where age gating is needed, we'll use respectful, privacy-preserving methods suited to a small, known community.

If stronger age assurance ever becomes legally required, we will use the **least invasive technology available** — including modern solutions like operating system-level age signals that confirm an age range without exposing your identity or personal data to us.

We are firmly opposed to mass surveillance infrastructure dressed up as child safety measures. If we are ever compelled to implement such systems, we will say so publicly.
