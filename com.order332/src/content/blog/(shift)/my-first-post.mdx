# Egg Recipes for Breakfast

Whether you prefer them scrambled, fried, poached, or in an omelet, eggs are a versatile and nutritious breakfast option. Here are a few easy egg-based recipes to try:

- Scrambled Eggs: Whisk eggs with a splash of milk, salt, and pepper, then cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until soft and fluffy.
- Fried Eggs: Heat a small amount of oil or butter in a skillet over medium heat. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and cook until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your desired doneness.
- Poached Eggs: Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Crack the eggs one at a time into the water and cook for 3-5 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
- Egg Omelet: Whisk eggs with your choice of fillings like cheese, vegetables, or meat. Pour into a hot, lightly oiled skillet and fold the omelet in half once the eggs have set.

Enjoy your eggs for a satisfying and nutritious start to the day!
