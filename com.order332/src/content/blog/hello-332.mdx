# Welcome to 332

332 is a private, invite-only community with roots in cybersecurity. We're global, we care about free speech, and we build things.

This site is the public face of what we do — a place to share projects, write about what we're working on, and document the things worth documenting.

## What We're Building

We have a few projects in the wild and more on the way:

- **Telos** — our multifunction Discord bot with an AI assistant and smart memory system
- **CTF Platform** — accessible through Telos at [ctf.order332.com](https://ctf.order332.com)
- **332 Labs** — coming soon. A platform integrating the CTF, courses, and media content
- **TwanOS** — a lightweight real-time separation kernel built by one of our members
